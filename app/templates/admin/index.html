Admin Portal

Here you can perform many different tasks. To go back to the form website click here.

To reaccess this page, use this url: https://flask-mtrainierneurology.herokuapp.com/admin and input the correct credentials.

Viewing Patients Records

To view the patient records in a quick copy and paste fashion click on the patient registration and patient follow-up tabs respectively.

Deleting Patients Records

To delete records, go to the form (represents the patient registration forms) and the follow up tabs, which brings you to a table.

On the left-hand side, check all the forms you want to delete, and then find the "with selected" button. Once you have this, click that and choose delete.

Once the data is deleted, it cannot be retrieved.

Other Information

You can also look at the table and sort the information or search if needed. You can manually create entries as well, though this may not be necessary.

You can also look at the user database, although I recommend only using one user for the office to make things simpler.
